I wrote a quick script a while ago to bruteforce English 5x5 Sator squares using a wordlist, and the only one that makes some limited sense is

  t i m i d
  i r a n i
  m a d a m
  i n a r i
  d i m i t
I.e. "[the] timid Iranian madam that Inari* dimit(=dismissed)".

* https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inari_%C5%8Ckami

(Of course, when googling for it somebody else had done the exact same thing and found the exact same square before: https://github.com/daniele-rapagnani/sator-squares)
