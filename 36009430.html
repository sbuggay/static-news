networked|parent|next|

I don't know what their actual business model is. The first thing Rooms.xyz makes me think of is Habbo (Hotel); it could sell extra features (through a subscription), premium items, and space to build in. A voxel editor and custom code make premium items a harder sell: you can replicate them if they aren't very complex and you don't care about the social status of using premium. The primary premium commodity could instead be space: larger rooms and more rooms per account. They could also charge users for highlighting their rooms in a recommendation system, if they cultivate a community that hangs put on the site.
