SVG is such an under-appreciated graphics format. I will expand his great quote about graphics:

> a two-dimensional representation to utilize the integrative capabilities of the human visual cortex

into:

SVG is a re-usable two-dimensional representation of data to utilize the integrative capabilities of the human visual cortex.

In a sense canvas, png/jpg etc are to SVG what PDF is to HTML. An information black hole.

As an example of the information-retaining quality of SVG representations you could fairly easily imagine the reverse operation: SVG Images TO Postgres.
