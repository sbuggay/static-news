It's a long video that deserves an introduction. This is the story of the creation of an artwork that was installed at the Hiroshima Museum of Contemporary Art. Most of the video is about the fabrication of the custom "Nixie tubes" (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nixie_tube) that are used. The video is simple, factual, and a wonderful example of extreme craftsmanship. If you'd like some text and still photos before you try the video, you can start here: https://www.daliborfarny.com/project/umashimenkana/.

vibrolax|prev|next|

I've been following this project for the past couple of years. The process of learning to manufacture enormous Nixie tubes as a product is a testament to Dalibor Farný's incredible dedication and skill. Who wouldn't like this succeed as a viable business? It's so niche, yet so cool.

One hopes that others who see this art work are inspired to use these incredible display tubes.
