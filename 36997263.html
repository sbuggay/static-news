Tangentially related is this tool for tuning the ECU in Cars. It helps you locate patterns in ECU rom dumps that are likely to be tables. Often they are sets of numbers in a 2D or 3D matrix of some dimension that have smooth increases. Partly it does this by showing you the values visually so you can spot them.
