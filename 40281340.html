sigmoid10|parent|next|

Light doesn't suddenly become free to travel anywhere inside the event horizon. At least not with our current understanding of general relativity. So once you fall behind the event horizon, you could see some light that is technically inside the horizon, but only light that fell in behind you. There's still no way to see the singularity (or anything closer to it than you), at least for static black holes.
