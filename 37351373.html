If I were writing this thesis I would have used the term "herb" rather than "ganja". I used to have a reggae program at a university radio station. One night I was interviewing a Rastafarian recording artist from Jamaica who was touring the US. He stopped me when I said "ganja".

"I would would never want to gan Jah", he said. After that I always used "herb" when speaking with Rastafarians. Ganja was not a word I used much anyway; I used the term "herb" with friends. Kids would always say "weed" but having studied so much reggae, I never thought of this as a weed. It's an herb.
