Open a terminal and then in bash:

    while true; do
        date;
        ./doit.sh;
        sleep 300;
    done | tee doit.log
if you want to get fancy, you can do:

    date "+%Y.%M.%dT%H:%m:%s-%Z`./doit.sh`"
to get it all on one line.
