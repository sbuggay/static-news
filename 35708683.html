When I was on my way back from the south pole a few years back, I sat next to a penguin researcher (not the author of this piece, but likely a colleague) on my flight between CHC and AKL. She goes every year and spends months with the penguins. The way she talked about them was extremely reverent and she seemed to revel in the extreme isolation. Not for me, but I can appreciate. I've also heard the penguin researchers don't smell great when coming back from the field, to the extent that the heli pilots don't like picking them up...
